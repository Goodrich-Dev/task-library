# Engage with social comments

**Use this when** posts are boosted (comments accrue for weeks on $1/day ads) and daily as standing routine across Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube, and the 44K group.

## Inputs
- Notification access to all channels where content runs (page, LinkedIn, YouTube, group)
- The owner's voice guide — replies publish under their name and must sound like them
- The content tracker, to route what comments produce (topics, testimonials, thank-you triggers)

## Steps
1. Check notifications on all boosted and recent organic posts **daily** — a $1/day ad keeps collecting comments long after posting.
2. Reply to every real comment within 24 hours in the owner's first-person voice: answer the question concretely, then ask one back to keep the thread alive. Threads are engagement; engagement raises relevance and **lowers cost per result** on the boost.
3. Like and pin the best comments; visible conversation is social proof for every later viewer the ad buys.
4. Harvest as you go: recurring questions → new one-minute video topics (feed the Topic Wheel / Produce stage); praise → screenshot for testimonials and Thank You Machine triggers; objections → future warm-stage content.
5. Handle negativity by rule: hide or report spam and abuse; **never delete honest criticism** — answer it publicly and specifically; a good public answer converts the audience watching.
6. Log the week's harvest (topics captured, testimonials saved, thank-yous triggered) in the MAA report.

## Definition of done (QA checklist)
- [ ] Every real comment across channels answered within 24 hours, in the owner's voice
- [ ] Best comments liked/pinned; spam hidden; honest criticism answered publicly, not deleted
- [ ] Harvest logged: new video topics, testimonial screenshots, Thank You Machine triggers
- [ ] Engagement effect visible in boost metrics (relevance, cost per result) week over week
- [ ] Linked back to the definitive article and relevant siblings
- [ ] Complies with Blog Posting Guidelines (if it publishes content)
