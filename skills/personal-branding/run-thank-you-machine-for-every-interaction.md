# Run Thank You Machine for every interaction

**Use this when** anyone does something worth acknowledging — a referral, review, introduction, piece of advice, or milestone — Phase 3 (Authority Building), run as a standing weekly system.

## Inputs
- A trigger log of meaningful interactions (milestones, referrals, reviews, advice, problem-solving moments)
- A phone for recording; access to social accounts and the ad account for boosting

## Steps
1. Identify the trigger: flag every moment worth acknowledging — milestones, referrals, reviews, good advice, someone solving a problem for you (/thank-you-machine).
2. Record a one-minute thank-you video on the phone, unscripted: name the person, say exactly what they did, explain why it mattered.
3. Process it through the Content Factory into a social post, blog excerpt, testimonial, ad creative, and email (/content-factory).
4. Post publicly and tag the person so they see it and share it with their audience — the share is the amplification.
5. Boost the best thank-yous with Dollar a Day at $1/day to reach people similar to those thanked (/dad).
6. Systematize it: add the thank-you step to the post-project checklist and block a weekly slot so gratitude runs on schedule, not on memory.

## Definition of done (QA checklist)
- [ ] Every meaningful interaction this week produced a recorded, posted thank-you
- [ ] Each video names the person, the act, and why it mattered; person tagged on social
- [ ] Best thank-yous processed through the Content Factory and boosted at $1/day
- [ ] Thank-you step lives in the post-project checklist and the weekly routine
- [ ] Linked back to the definitive article and relevant siblings
- [ ] Complies with Blog Posting Guidelines (if it publishes content)

## Run on a persistent agent (Fable 5)
A persistent agent (Claude Fable 5 or a comparable OpenAI/Google model) makes the weekly system real: it watches the trigger log, drafts each thank-you brief for the person to record, runs the Content Factory and $1/day boost steps, and loops until the week's Definition-of-done fully passes — every meaningful interaction acknowledged — not 90%.
It self-verifies each post is live, the person tagged, and the boost staged before closing the week.
Gratitude runs on schedule because the agent persists, not on memory of a human; triggers and outcomes accrue in memory alongside the Authority-phase validation inventory, and a meta-article example is logged each run so the brand compounds.
